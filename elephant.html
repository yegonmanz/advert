>>The elephant is the largest land animal on Earth. It is known for its long trunk, tusks, and large ears. Elephants are herbivores, feeding mainly on leaves, grasses, bamboo, and fruits. They are highly intelligent and social animals, living in groups called herds. Elephants have a strong sense of family and are known for their deep emotional bonds. Unfortunately, elephants are threatened by habitat loss, poaching for their tusks, and human-wildlife conflict. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect these majestic creatures for future generations.
